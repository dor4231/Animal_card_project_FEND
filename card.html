Swiss Shepherd

The White Swiss Shepherd Dog is known for its good demeanor towards children.

  • Color: White!
  • Size: Medium to Larg dog
  • Working Dog: Guard Dog, Sporting Dog
  • Country Of Origin: Switzerland

The White Swiss Shepherd Dog, known as the Berger Blanc Suisse in Switzerland, is famous for sharing a nature that is nearly identical to that of the German Shepherd. These dogs are alert and loyal pets with lots of energy to go around. They love a challenge and need the exercise in doing so. Regular exercise is essential to maintaining their energy as well, as otherwise they may take it out on your house or your front yard.
